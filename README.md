🚀 SENDIV — Export Business Blueprint

SENDIV is a modern, visually rich web-based guide designed to help beginners in India start an export business from scratch.
It provides a step-by-step roadmap from product selection to getting your first international order.

⸻

🌐 Live Concept

This project represents a demo export learning platform UI with structured chapters, actionable steps, and real-world guidance.

⸻

📌 Features

* 📖 13 Structured Chapters
* 📊 Step-by-step Export Business Guide
* 📋 Interactive Document Checklist
* 💼 Legal Registration Workflow (IEC, GST, MSME)
* 🌍 International Buyer Finding Strategies
* 💰 Pricing & Deal Closing Methods
* 🚚 Logistics & Shipping Guidance
* 📅 30-Day Execution Plan
* 📄 Ready-to-use Export Templates

⸻

🧠 What You Learn

* How to choose profitable export products
* How to register your export business in India
* Required documents for international trade
* How to find and contact foreign buyers
* Pricing strategies (FOB, CIF)
* Payment methods & fraud prevention
* Shipping and logistics basics

⸻

🛠️ Tech Stack
